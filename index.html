ORBITZ

By David Campos R.

Being in my first year of career I had the idea of a game with balls who would orbitate around planets and compite between them to stand in the orbit. That same year, after a week of hard work I had already the first version of the game. I found the mechanics so funny, but the game fell into oblivion.

Three years later, when I discover the Three Headed Monkey Awards, I decide this could be a good place to show the project to the world. Unfortunately, the contest application time was ending in only five days. With the colaboration of my flatmate, Paco, we worked hard to get a good version to present. And here is to you.
